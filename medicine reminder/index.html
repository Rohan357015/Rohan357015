About Our Mission

At Save Animals Now, we believe that every injured animal deserves a second chance at life. Our mission is to connect people who find injured animals with NGOs that specialize in animal rescue and rehabilitation. By collaborating with these NGOs, we ensure quick action to transport animals to nearby veterinary hospitals, providing them with the care they urgently need.

Our website is a platform where people can report injured animals in their localities. Once a report is submitted, it is immediately sent to our partner NGOs, whose teams respond swiftly to ensure the animal is rescued and receives proper medical treatment.

Why Choose Us?

  • We work with the best animal NGOs in the country, guaranteeing a fast response.
  • Our platform is easy to use and helps you report animal emergencies in just a few clicks.
  • We ensure the safe transport of injured animals to nearby hospitals for immediate treatment.

Join us in our mission to protect animals. Whether you want to report an injured animal, donate to support our cause, or learn more about our NGO partners, Save Animals Now is here to make the process easy and effective.
